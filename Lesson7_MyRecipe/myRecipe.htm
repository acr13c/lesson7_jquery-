Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil, for tossing
  • 5 Sweet Potatoes
  • 1 tablesppon House Seasoning
  • 1/2 t paprika
  • 1 c salt
  • 1/4 c black pepper
  • 1/4 c garlic powder

Instructions

Preheat oven to 450.

Line a sheet tray with parchment.

In a large bowl toss sweet potatoes just enough oil to coat.

Sprinkle with House Seasoning and paprika.

Spread sweet potatoes in single layer on prepared baking sheet, being sure not to overcrowd.

Bake until sweet potatoes are tender and golden brown, turning occasionally, about 20 minutes.

Let cool 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
